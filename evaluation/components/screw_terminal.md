## Aliexpress

1. KF301-5.0-2P (The "Standard" Blue Block)

    This is the most direct equivalent. It is the chunky, usually blue (sometimes green or black) terminal block found in most hobbyist kits.

    AliExpress Search: KF301 5.0 2P

    Key Specs: 5.0mm Pitch, 2 Pins, ~16A rating.

2. KF128-5.0-2P (The Slim Green Block)

    If you want the slimmer, bright green version often used on industrial boards, use this model number. It has a slightly smaller footprint but fits the same 5mm hole spacing.

    AliExpress Search: KF128 5.0 2P

    Key Specs: 5.0mm Pitch, 2 Pins, ~10A rating.

3. DG128 / WJ128 (Industrial Equivalents)

    These are high-quality alternatives often manufactured by brands like Degson. They are physically interchangeable with the KF128.

    AliExpress Search: DG128 5.0 2P or WJ128 5.0 2P
